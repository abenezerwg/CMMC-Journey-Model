## The 6 Stages

1. **DISCOVER** - Business case & obligations
2. **SCOPE** - Define boundaries & inventory
3. **ASSESS** - Gap analysis & scoring
4. **IMPLEMENT** - Control deployment
5. **DOCUMENT** - SSP & evidence
6. **CERTIFY** - C3PAO assessment
