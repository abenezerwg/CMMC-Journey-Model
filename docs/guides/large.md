# CMMC Journey Path: Large Enterprise (250+ employees)

⚠️ **Disclaimer:** This is an unofficial, open-source guide. Use for educational purposes only.

## Overview
- Complex multi-site scope
- Advanced C3PAO required
- 12-month timeline
- $150K+ budget
- Tier 1 + Tier 2 fully, Tier 3 prioritized, Tier 4 optional

## Recommended Stages

### 1. Discover
- Executive alignment
- Contract analysis and obligations mapping
- ROI and risk assessment

### 2. Scope
- Full asset inventory
- CUI/FCI classification across sites
- Enclave planning
- Multi-system boundaries

### 3. Assess
- Comprehensive gap analysis
- POA&M development with prioritization
- Cross-department risk review

### 4. Implement
- Critical and essential controls first
- Tier 3 controls in parallel
- Evidence collection, policy updates, staff training at scale

### 5. Document
- SSP covering all systems and sites
- Final POA&M
- Evidence repository for C3PAO review

### 6. Certify
- C3PAO selection
- Pre-assessment and remediation cycles
- Formal assessment and ongoing compliance

## Tips
- Use enterprise project management and collaboration tools
- Assign clear ownership per site and department
- Plan for complex change management and training
