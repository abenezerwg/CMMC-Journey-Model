# CMMC Journey Path: Large Enterprise (250+ employees)

⚠️ **Disclaimer:** This is an unofficial, open-source guide. Use for educational purposes only.

## Overview
- Complex multi-site scope with multiple departments
- Certified CMMC Assessor (CCA) required for assessment and certification
- **18–24 month timeline** (typical for large organizations; realistic planning critical)
- $150K–$300K+ budget (including C3PAO assessment, tools, consulting, training)
- **Level 1 + Level 2 fully, Level 3 controls prioritized across all sites**

**Note:** CMMC has three levels: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3. There is no Level 4.

## Recommended Stages

### 1. Discover
- Executive alignment across organization
- Comprehensive contract analysis for all CMMC obligations
- ROI and risk assessment including multi-site considerations
- Budget and resource planning at enterprise scale

### 2. Scope
- Full asset inventory across all sites and departments
- CUI/FCI classification and data flow mapping
- Multi-site enclave planning and network segmentation
- Assessment boundary definition per site and system

### 3. Assess
- Comprehensive gap analysis of all **Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 controls**
- Verification against **320+ Assessment Objectives (AOs)** at scale
- POA&M development with enterprise-wide prioritization
- Cross-department and cross-site risk review

### 4. Implement
- **Level 1 critical controls first** across all sites
- Parallel Level 2 and Level 3 control implementation
- Evidence collection, policy updates, staff training at scale
- Establish enterprise-wide audit logging, monitoring, and reporting

### 5. Document
- SSP covering all systems, sites, and departments
- Final POA&M with realistic milestones and resource allocation
- Evidence repository organized by control family and site
- Supporting documentation (procedures, policies, diagrams, configurations)

### 6. Certify
- Engage **Certified CMMC Assessor (CCA)** for pre-assessment validation
- C3PAO selection (recommend experienced team for complex scope)
- C3PAO assessment across all sites (typically 10+ days on-site)
- Address assessment findings and remediate
- Continuous compliance program for 3-year certification validity

## Tips
- Use enterprise project management and collaboration tools
- Assign clear ownership per site and department with escalation paths
- Plan for complex change management and organization-wide training
- Establish steering committee with executive and IT leadership
- Implement centralized monitoring and reporting for compliance tracking
- POA&M acceptance: Realistic multi-phase approach with executive sign-off on timeline and budget
